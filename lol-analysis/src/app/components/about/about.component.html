What is LoL Picker?
LoL Picker or League of Legends Picker is a tool which suggests the best fitting champions for you based on what you want more - to counter the enemy or have synergy with your team composition
What should I choose?
Choose counters if you:
didn't get your main role
or
are playing as a carry
Choose synergy if you:
got your main role
or
are playing as a support
How to use
If you chose counters just input currently known enemy champions and hit find. You will see all suggested champions for all lanes.
If you chose synergy input currently known teammate champions and leave your role blank. After that you will see all suggested champions for lanes that you left blank
